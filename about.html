Our
Story:

Begins with the Love for Indie Films

Square Eyes is a platform for discovery, creativity, and connection. Frustrated by the dominance of big-budget blockbusters, our founders set out to create a space where films that break the mold could thrive. We give independent filmmakers a stage to showcase their work and provide audiences with unique stories that challenge conventions and inspire thought.

At Square Eyes, we champion the dreamers and risk-takers—creators who craft bold, authentic films you won’t find anywhere else. Our curated collection offers films that captivate, move, and make you think, bringing fresh perspectives and unheard voices to the forefront.

When you browse through Square Eyes, we offer films that captivate the imagination, films that stir emotions, and films that make you think long after the credits roll. Here, you’ll find new perspectives and unheard voices, each one offering something truly special.

Our Mission:

Every film you watch on Square Eyes is more than just entertainment; it’s a way to support passionate creators and bring their stories to life. Our mission is to create a community where indie filmmakers can showcase their work and connect with viewers who are eager for original, creative films. Every purchase directly supports filmmakers, allowing them to continue telling the stories that matter.
