Our Mission:

Every film you watch on Square Eyes is more than just entertainment; it's a way to support passionate creators and bring their stories to life. Our mission is to create a thriving community where indie filmmakers can showcase their work and connect with viewers who are eager for original, creative films that push boundaries and explore new territories.

Every purchase directly supports filmmakers, enabling them to continue telling the stories that matter and funding their next creative endeavors. We believe in the power of cinema to change perspectives, spark conversations, and build bridges between diverse communities around the world.
