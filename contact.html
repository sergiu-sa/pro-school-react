Got a questions? We got answers! Check out our FAQ section for answers.

01 What is Square Eyes?
Square Eyes is a streaming service that offers a curated collection of independent films and documentaries from around the world. Our mission is to support independent filmmakers and bring unique stories to our audience.
02 How much does Square Eyes cost?
Square Eyes offers multiple subscription plans to fit your budget. You can choose a monthly, quarterly, or annual plan, with discounts available for long-term subscriptions. Check our pricing page for the latest details.
03 What content is available on Square Eyes?
Our platform offers a wide range of independent films, including genres like drama, comedy, horror, documentary, and more. We focus on diverse, lesser-known films that provide fresh perspectives and storytelling.
04 How can i watch Square Eyes?
SYou can watch Square Eyes on any internet-connected device. We support smartphones, tablets, computers, and smart TVs. Simply download our app or access the website and log into your account.
05 How do i sign up for Square Eyes?
To sign up, click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. You’ll be prompted to create an account and choose a subscription plan. After payment, you’ll have immediate access to our library.
06 What is the Square Eyes free trial?
We offer a 7-day free trial for new users to explore our library and see if Square Eyes is right for them. You can cancel anytime during the trial without being charged.
07 How do i contact Square Eyes customer support?
Our support team is here to help! You can reach us via the "Contact" page on our website, where you’ll find a form to submit your questions. Alternatively, email us at support@squareeyes.com.
08 What are the payments methods accepted by Square Eyes?
We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. We also plan to add more payment options in the future.
