Got a question? We got answers! Check out our FAQ section for quick solutions to common questions about Square Eyes.

01 What is Square Eyes?
Square Eyes is a streaming service that offers a curated collection of independent films and documentaries from around the world. Our mission is to support independent filmmakers and bring unique stories to our audience while connecting film lovers globally.
02 How much does Square Eyes cost?
Square Eyes offers multiple subscription plans to fit your budget. You can choose a monthly, quarterly, or annual plan, with discounts available for long-term subscriptions. We also offer individual movie rentals and purchases. Check our pricing page for the latest details.
03 What content is available on Square Eyes?
Our platform offers a wide range of independent films, including genres like drama, comedy, horror, documentary, thriller, sci-fi, and more. We focus on diverse, lesser-known films that provide fresh perspectives and innovative storytelling from passionate independent filmmakers.
04 How can I watch Square Eyes?
You can watch Square Eyes on any internet-connected device. We support smartphones, tablets, computers, smart TVs, and gaming consoles. Simply download our app from your device's app store or access our website and log into your account to start watching.
05 How do I sign up for Square Eyes?
To sign up, click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. You'll be prompted to create an account with your email address and choose a subscription plan. After completing payment, you'll have immediate access to our entire library of independent films and documentaries.
06 What is the Square Eyes free trial?
We offer a 7-day free trial for new users to explore our library and experience the unique world of independent cinema. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged. The trial gives you full access to all features and content.
07 How do I contact Square Eyes customer support?
Our support team is here to help! You can reach us via the contact form on this page, where you can submit your questions or issues. Alternatively, email us directly at support@squareeyes.com. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.
08 What payment methods does Square Eyes accept?
We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and PayPal. For Norwegian customers, we also accept Vipps. We're constantly working to add more convenient payment options.
09 Can I download movies for offline viewing?
Yes! Square Eyes Premium subscribers can download select movies and documentaries for offline viewing. Downloads are available for up to 30 days and must be watched within 48 hours once you start playback. This feature is perfect for traveling or areas with limited internet.
10 How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your Square Eyes subscription at any time through your account settings. Simply log in, go to "Manage Subscription," and click "Cancel Subscription." You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees apply.
